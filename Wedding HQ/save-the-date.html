Designer's Pick

Option A — “La Nuit”

This one earns its keep before anyone reads a word. A black save-the-date arrives in a mailbox and says black-tie mandatory without saying anything. The gold foil letterforms against noir are exactly the Old Hollywood Gatsby register you named — they read expensive, cinematic, and unrepeatable. Option B is beautiful but risks landing closer to every luxury wedding done in the last decade. Option A stakes a claim. The oval monogram reads like it belongs to a heritage fashion house. Go dark, go bold, go once-in-a-lifetime.
