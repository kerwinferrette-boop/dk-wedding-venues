Designer's Pick

Option A — “Noir Editorial”

The contrast logic of Option A is editorial and intentional: the black invite signals the event's register from across the room, and then the ivory RSVP card is the exhale — the softer, more intimate moment between the couple and their guest. Option B is technically more traditional and there's nothing wrong with it, but traditional is not the word this wedding is looking for. The gold letterpress on noir invite is the flex. The ivory RSVP card with its Art Deco frame is the grace note. That pairing is why Option A wins.
