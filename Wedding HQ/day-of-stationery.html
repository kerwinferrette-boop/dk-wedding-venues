Table Card Note

Both colorways ship as a set — alternate Ivory and Noir across tables for a composed, editorial room. Or go all-noir for maximum drama. Both are sized at 4×6 in, designed to stand in acrylic table holders.

Naming the Tables

Consider naming tables after La Valencia rooms or La Jolla landmarks instead of numbers: The Med, The Belvedere, La Jolla Cove, Torrey Pines, Sunset Cliffs. Tell your story through the room.
