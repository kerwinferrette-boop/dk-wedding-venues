A Note on Mixing

THE HYBRID APPROACH

These don't have to live in separate worlds. Consider: Direction A for the Save the Date and formal invitation suite (pre-event, sets the black-tie tone), and Direction B for the day-of pieces — table numbers, welcome sign, menus — which guests experience inside the actual La Valencia setting. The colors of the hotel surround them. The stationery echoes it.

THE UNIFIER

The logo — the crowned KD crest — is the constant. It reads on noir, on Pacific navy, on stucco blush, on Mediterranean cream. Every piece, both directions, anchors to the same mark. That's what makes a hybrid approach cohesive rather than chaotic.
